    How to Play

    Goal

    Score the most points by placing tiles that share a color or shape with their neighbors.

    Tiles

    6 shapes × 6 colors. Each unique tile appears 3 times in the bag. You hold 6 tiles at a time.

    Placing Tiles

    • Click a tile in your hand to select it.
    • Valid board cells will highlight — click one to stage the tile.
    • All tiles played in one turn must share the same row or column.
    • Each line shares exactly one attribute: all same color (different shapes) or all same shape (different colors).
    • No duplicate tiles in a line.
    • Tiles must connect to the existing board (except the first play).

    Scoring

    • Each tile scores the length of every line it contributes to.
    • Completing a line of 6 earns a 6-point bonus.
    • Emptying your hand when the bag is empty: +6 points.

    Actions

    • Play — commit staged tiles and end your turn.
    • Swap — select hand tiles to exchange with the bag (skips turn).
    • Pass — skip your turn. All players passing ends the game.

    End Game

    Game ends when the bag empties and a player empties their hand, or all players pass consecutively. Highest score wins.
