    HOW TO PLAY

    Goal

    Score the most points by placing tiles that share a color or shape with their neighbors.

    Tiles

    There are 6 shapes and 6 colors. Each unique combination appears 3 times in the bag. You hold 6 tiles at a time.

    Placing Tiles

    • Click a tile in your hand to select it.
    • Click a highlighted cell on the board to place it.
    • All tiles played in one turn must be in the same row or column.
    • Each line on the board shares exactly one attribute — either all the same color (different shapes) or all the same shape (different colors).
    • No duplicate tiles in a line.
    • Placed tiles must connect to the existing board (except the very first play).

    Scoring

    • Each tile scores points equal to the length of every line it contributes to.
    • Completing a line of 6 tiles earns a 6-point bonus.
    • Emptying your hand when the bag is empty earns +6 points.

    Actions

    • Play — commit your staged tiles and end your turn.
    • Swap — select tiles from your hand to exchange with the bag (skips your turn).
    • Pass — skip your turn. If all players pass in a row, the game ends.

    End Game

    The game ends when the bag is empty and a player empties their hand, or when all players pass consecutively. Highest score wins.
